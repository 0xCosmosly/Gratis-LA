## KEY FINDING

**Pattern Observed:** Yelp's algorithm for "No Tipping Restaurant" essentially filters by establishments with **automatic service charges** rather than genuinely tip-free establishments without surcharges.

The search term "No Tipping Restaurant" on Yelp is misleading - it primarily surfaces restaurants that ADVERTISE having no tipping policy BECAUSE they have mandatory service charges built into their bills.

---

## METHODOLOGY

For each restaurant where the preview comment didn't clearly indicate their tipping policy:
1. Navigated to individual restaurant detail page
2. Scrolled to reviews section
3. Clicked on "Search reviews" textbox
4. Typed "tip" and clicked Search
5. Analyzed results to determine if:
   - Has NO tipping AND NO service charge = TRUE POSITIVE ✓
   - Has NO tipping BUT HAS service charge = FALSE POSITIVE ✗
   - Accepts tips OR unclear = NEUTRAL/EXCLUDED
